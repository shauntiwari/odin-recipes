Chicken Parmesan

Description

I love chicken parm, especially when it's made with fresh mozzarella, which it almost never is in restaurants.

Of course, at home we can use the real stuff, but it can be pricey. So I tried something new--a cheese spread using ricotta fortified with sharp Cheddar.

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts
  • egg
  • crumbs of bread
  • ricotta cheese
  • cheddar cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • olive oil
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. preheat oven
  2. pound chicken
  3. heat oil
  4. mix cheeses
  5. bake in oven
  6. serve on plates
